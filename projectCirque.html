Cirque de la Lune Branding

Cirque de la Lune is a fictitious circus that I have created a branding identity for. The pieces designed include a poster; featuring Doris the bear and Bobby the chimp working together to pull off a balancing stunt. Other animals join the duo, evident in the invitiation and it's envelope, the two treat bags, the set of 4 enamel pins, and the set of 4 animal masks. The color scheme includes that of beige, black, white, and red. The animals don a crayon, scribble-like texture, with plenty of stars to appeal to the children, but a sense of innocence to attract passerbys.
