Laters Café

A project revolving around flushing out the identity of a fictitious cat-themed café, located in the wonderful city of Burlington, Vermont. Branding Identity features a quaint and playful logo of a sugar-cube cat, warming up in a cup of tea, with hand-drawn type propped up ontop of the saucer, with the S as the cup's handle. The pieces that create this brand boast a minimalistic color palette; that of white, dark gray, tan, and orange. The mascots of the brand are of mischievious-looking cats, which help tie the brand together. The identity for Laters Cafe includes a logo, the front and back of a business card, a letterhead, two articles of clothing, coasters, the packaging of a matchbox, salt and pepper labels, two cups, and a paper bag.
