100 Acre Wood Map

A personal rendition of Winnie the Pooh's 100 Acre Wood Map in the theme; SCI-FI. The map features 2D illustrations that simulate the appearance of an old retro video-game user interface. Each of the characters have been illustrated to fit the theme, with topographic lines running through to signify elevation and depth. The blue silhouettes that are filled in by grid textures are to mimic the effect of a scanner that picks up forms of life.
