Killer Bees Survival Kit

A branding identity I designed for a fictitious survival kit that may aid the user in fending off killer bees. This design sports a buzzingly active lifestyle, revolving around the theme of health. The visual tie between each component of the brand revolves around honey. The case is designed in the shape of a hive, the pockets come in the shape of hexagons to mimic the interior of a hive. Essential oils, sneakers, goggles, dryer sheets, an olympics style of torch, a pair of tweezers, and a handy pamphlet on how to use each piece are dotted amongst the interior of the kit, in the case of a sudden horde of killer bees.
