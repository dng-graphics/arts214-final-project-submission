Dooms Day Tarot Series

The Dooms Day Tarot Card series is a fictional drink line designed to suit the "magic potions" theme. The idea behind the project was to create 6 fictitious flavors as a series for the fictitious brewing company. The labels for the drinks are stylized in a very earthy, foliage-strewn composition, donning the colors dandelion yellow, fuschia, white, and black. Each of the different flavors are encased in a tarot card relevant to their theme. The foliage comes to extend beyond the card and roots itself, bringing the rest of the label together. The packaging is designed to portray the flavors as cards in a hand, as well as the idea of having acquired a collection.
